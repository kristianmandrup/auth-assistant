This gem has been designed for Rails 3 only. Any back port to Rails 2 is most welcome ;)

Insert <pre>gem 'auth-assistant'</pre> in your Rails 3 _Gemfile_

<pre>$ bundle install</pre>

Run the "config generator":http://wiki.github.com/kristianmandrup/auth-assistant/config-generator to configure *auth_assistant* with a  "role strategy":http://wiki.github.com/kristianmandrup/auth-assistant/role-strategies for use with "cancan":http://github.com/ryanb/cancan and "devise":http://github.com/plataformatec/devise 

Finally make the group of *auth_assistant* "view helpers":http://wiki.github.com/kristianmandrup/auth-assistant/view-helpers accessible in your views, by adding them to the *ApplicationHelper* module like this:

<pre>
module ApplicationHelper       
  auth_assist_helpers
end</pre>
